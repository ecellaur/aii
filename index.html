About Us

Enterpreneurship cell is an Community which help the Students.
The Entrepreneurial body, E Cell of the Amity University, Rajasthan (AUR), commonly known as Amity innovation Incubator (AII) has emerged as a platform for all the innovative minds out there, seeking a proper guidance to contribute in bringing a better future for the nation. E-Cell of AUR works to spread the spirit of entrepreneurship among the young minds who have great zeal to do something, which would make them stand apart from the crowd. Since it's inaugral, it has been working to inspire and ignite the youth to walk on the path, which their passion points towards and provides them with the best of support.
The Incubator works with three stages in mind which are: Ideate, Innovate, Implement, which has proven to be the most decent strategy to help any idea to turn into a revolutionary step.
